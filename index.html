Gesture Cheatsheet

Live Controls

  • Index + middle finger: hover over the top bar to pick a color or erase.
  • Index + middle finger over the gesture dock: switch brush mode, background, undo, redo, replay, or start challenges.
  • Index finger only: draw in the air.
  • Thumb + index pinch: resize your brush.
  • Open palm: hold to clear the whole canvas.
  • Thumbs up: hold to save a PNG snapshot.
  • Rock sign: hold to trigger the next challenge prompt.
